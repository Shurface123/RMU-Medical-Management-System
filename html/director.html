Chief Medical Officer

Dr. Emmanuel Mensah

Chief Medical Officer, RMU Medical Sickbay

Dr. Emmanuel Mensah serves as the Chief Medical Officer of RMU Medical Sickbay, bringing over 20 years of experience in university healthcare and medical administration. He oversees all medical operations and ensures the delivery of quality healthcare services to the RMU community. Under his leadership, the sickbay has expanded its services, modernised its facilities, and improved healthcare outcomes for thousands of students and staff.

Qualifications

  • MBChB — University of Ghana Medical School
  • MPH — Johns Hopkins University, USA
  • Fellow — Ghana College of Physicians and Surgeons

Key Achievements

Spearheaded the digitisation of the sickbay's medical records management system
Expanded sickbay services to include mental health counselling and physiotherapy
Secured accreditation renewal from the Medical and Dental Council of Ghana
Published research on university healthcare delivery in sub-Saharan Africa
Director's Message

A Message from the CMO

"As the Chief Medical Officer of the RMU Medical Sickbay, I am committed to ensuring that every student, faculty member, and staff person receives the highest standard of healthcare. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to provide compassionate, accessible, and evidence-based care. Your health is not just our duty — it is our calling."

Dr. Emmanuel Mensah
