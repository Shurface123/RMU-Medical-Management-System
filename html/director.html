Dr. Emmanuel Mensah

Chief Medical Officer

Dr. Emmanuel Mensah serves as the Chief Medical Officer of RMU Medical Sickbay, bringing over 20 years of experience in university healthcare and medical administration. He oversees all medical operations and ensures the delivery of quality healthcare services to the RMU community.

Qualifications

  • MBChB - University of Ghana Medical School
  • MPH - Johns Hopkins University
  • Fellow, Ghana College of Physicians

Office Hours: Monday - Friday, 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Email: director@rmu-medical.edu.gh

Message from the Director

"Welcome to RMU Medical Sickbay. Our commitment is to provide exceptional healthcare services that support your academic journey and personal well-being. We understand that good health is fundamental to academic success, and our team is dedicated to ensuring that every member of the RMU community has access to quality medical care when they need it."

"Whether you need routine checkups, emergency care, or health counseling, our doors are always open. We continuously strive to improve our services and welcome your feedback as we work together to build a healthier campus community."

Dr. Emmanuel Mensah
Chief Medical Officer
