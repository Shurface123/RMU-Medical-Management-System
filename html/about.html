About RMU Medical Sickbay

Committed to excellence in healthcare for over two decades

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality healthcare services to the Regional Maritime University community, ensuring the physical and mental well-being of students, faculty, and staff through compassionate care, preventive medicine, and health education.

Our Vision

To be the leading university health facility in Ghana, recognized for excellence in student healthcare, innovative medical practices, and creating a healthy campus environment that supports academic success and personal development.

Our Story

The RMU Medical Sickbay was established alongside Regional Maritime University to ensure that students, faculty, and staff have access to quality healthcare services without leaving campus. For over 20 years, we have been dedicated to maintaining the health and wellness of the RMU community.

Our facility operates under the supervision of a Chief Medical Officer and is staffed by qualified doctors, nurses, and support personnel who are committed to providing exceptional care. We understand the unique healthcare needs of a university community and have tailored our services accordingly.

The Medical Sickbay provides twenty-four hour service, ensuring that emergency medical care is always available. For students, we cover all expenses related to treatment, while faculty and staff receive comprehensive health-related advice and referrals when necessary.
