About RMU Medical Sickbay

Excellence in University Healthcare

Committed to serving the health and wellness of the RMU community for over two decades

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality healthcare to the Regional Maritime University community, ensuring the physical and mental well-being of students, faculty, and staff through compassionate care, preventive medicine, and health education.

Our Vision

To be the leading university health facility in Ghana, recognised for excellence in student healthcare, innovative medical practices, and creating a healthy campus environment that supports academic success and personal development.

Our Values

Compassion, Excellence, Integrity, Accessibility, Innovation, and Collaboration. These six values guide every interaction, decision, and service delivery across all our departments.

Our Story

Two Decades of Healthcare Excellence

The RMU Medical Sickbay was established alongside Regional Maritime University to ensure that students, faculty, and staff have access to quality healthcare services without leaving campus. For over 20 years, we have been dedicated to maintaining the health and wellness of the RMU community.

Our facility operates under the supervision of a Chief Medical Officer and is staffed by qualified doctors, nurses, and support personnel committed to providing exceptional care. We understand the unique healthcare needs of a university community and have tailored our services accordingly.

The Medical Sickbay provides 24-hour service, ensuring that emergency medical care is always available. For students, we cover all expenses related to treatment, while faculty and staff receive health advice and referrals when necessary.
