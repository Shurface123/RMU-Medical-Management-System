About Us

Comprehensive Healthcare for the RMU Community

Regional Maritime University Medical Sickbay has been serving the university community for over two decades. We provide comprehensive healthcare services to students, faculty, and staff with a commitment to excellence, compassion, and accessibility.

  • 24/7 Emergency Services & Ambulance
  • Free & Subsidised Checkups for Students
  • Board-Certified Doctors & Experienced Nurses
  • Modern Laboratory & Diagnostic Equipment
