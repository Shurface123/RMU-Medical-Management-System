Departments

Our Specialist Divisions

Nursing Division

Our nursing team is the heart of the sickbay, providing round-the-clock patient care, administering medications, and coordinating with doctors on treatment plans.

Pharmacy Department

Licensed pharmacists ensure safe dispensing of prescription medication, manage drug inventory, and provide patient counselling on drug usage.

Laboratory Services

Qualified medical laboratory scientists conduct diagnostic tests with precision and accuracy, delivering results within 24 to 48 hours.

Ambulance & Emergency

Our trained paramedics and ambulance drivers provide rapid emergency response anywhere on camp us, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Administration

Our administrative staff handle patient registration, appointments, records management, billing, and overall operational coordination.

Mental Health Support

Trained counsellors and mental health professionals provide confidential support for students experiencing academic stress, anxiety or depression.
