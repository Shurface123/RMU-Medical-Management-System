FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our services

What are the sickbay opening hours?
The RMU Medical Sickbay operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week for emergency services. Regular consultation hours are Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
Is the sickbay free for students?
Healthcare services at the RMU Sickbay are heavily subsidised for all registered students. Primary care services are generally provided at no direct cost. Some specialist services may attract a nominal fee.
How do I get my lab results?
Lab results are typically available within 24–48 hours. For registered system users, results can be viewed through your patient dashboard. You can also collect them in person at the laboratory.
Can I get my prescriptions refilled?
Yes. Prescription refills require a follow-up consultation with the attending physician. Please bring your original prescription or medical records to your appointment.
What should I do in a medical emergency?
In a life-threatening emergency, call our emergency hotline immediately: 153. You can also proceed directly to the sickbay. Our ambulance service is available 24/7 to respond to campus emergencies.
Can staff members use the sickbay?
Yes. All staff members of Regional Maritime University are entitled to use sickbay services. Some services may require a valid staff identification card.
