24/7 Emergency Care

Our emergency department is open round-the-clock to handle all medical emergencies. We have trained emergency physicians and nurses ready to provide immediate care for critical situations.

  • Immediate medical attention
  • Trauma care and stabilization
  • Emergency medications available
  • Direct admission to bed facilities if needed
